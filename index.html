Art of Finding

Welcome to Art of Finding

Your one stop shop to fullfill your public artpiece finding needs.

Using Art of Finding is simple. Enter an Artists name above. Choose a piece of art from this carousel. Information on the artpiece is inside the card below along with a map showing where the artpiece is located. If you grab the guy in the map and place it on the location it will take you inside of where the artpiece is located to get a closer look at the actual piece. That's it. Ready to get started? Happy Art Finding!
